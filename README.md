# Automated Building Upgrade (CK3)

A quality-of-life mod that adds a **Decisions** entry to upgrade every eligible building in your personal domain in one click.

## Requirements

- Crusader Kings III **1.19.x**
- No DLC required

## Installation

1. Copy `automated_building_upgrade.mod` to your CK3 mod folder:
   - **Windows:** `Documents\Paradox Interactive\Crusader Kings III\mod\`
   - **Linux:** `~/.local/share/Paradox Interactive/Crusader Kings III/mod/`
2. Copy the `automated_building_upgrade` folder to:
   - **Windows:** `Documents\Paradox Interactive\Crusader Kings III\mod\automated_building_upgrade\`
   - **Linux:** `~/.local/share/Paradox Interactive/Crusader Kings III/mod/automated_building_upgrade/`
3. Enable the mod in the CK3 launcher.

## How to use

Open **Decisions** and choose **Upgrade Domain Buildings**. The decision upgrades every eligible building in your **direct holdings** as far as your current innovations and gold allow, stopping at the highest tier you qualify for today.

### What is included

- Main holdings (castles, cities, temples, tribal holdings)
- Standard economic, fortification, and military slot buildings

### What is excluded

- Vassal holdings
- Duchy capital buildings
- Special buildings
- Nomadic yurt / domicile buildings

Upgrades are instant. Provinces with ongoing construction are skipped. Each use raises buildings as far as your innovations, requirements, and gold allow — capital county is upgraded first when gold is tight.
